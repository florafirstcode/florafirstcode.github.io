Summer Palace Island Shangri-La

Delicious Cantonese cuisine with two Michelin stars. Totally worth the treat! The barbecued suckling pig is a classic. Great for dimsum as well- love the rice rolls ("cheung fun") with crispy shrimp inside. Don't forget to save room for dessert!

Address: Island Shangri-La Hotel, Level 5, Admiralty
Phone: 2820 8552

Fu Sing

My all time favorite place for good quality BBQ pork. I crave their BBQ pork buns- especially when I'm abroad and thinking about what I want for my first meal back. Their piping hot BBQ bun with the perfectly crispy crust is a must! Fu Sing also serves a lot of other good Chinese classics (rice, noodles, meats, seafood, veggies, etc). Get the deep freid diced tofu, soy sauce chicken, and pea shoots in soup.

Address: 1/F, Grand Millennium Plaza, 181 Queen's Road Central, Sheung Wan
Phone: 2643 3228
